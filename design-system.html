Brand Overview

Ψ

Luxury travel & hospitality

The brand uses a trident-style logomark (Ψ) in Black Coal against Saffron Yellow. The aesthetic communicates premium luxury travel with warmth, clarity, and confidence — refined yet approachable, never cold or overly minimal.

Color Usage Rules

  • Saffron Yellow is the hero color — primary CTAs, active/selected states, branded headers, active filter chips.
  • Black Coal for primary text, headings, filled dark buttons, and icon buttons.
  • White as text on dark fills; default card and surface color.
  • Semantic colors (Green, Red, Orange) are only for validation states and status tags — never decorative.
  • Text on Saffron Yellow must be Black Coal. Text on Black Coal must be White.
  • China branding colors (Blue Wave, Marigold) are restricted to the China market only.

Accessibility Guidelines

⌨All interactive elements must have visible focus indicators.
🎨Color alone must never convey meaning — always pair semantic colors with icons (✓ success, ✗ error, ⚠ warning).
🔍Minimum contrast ratios: 4.5:1 for normal text, 3:1 for large text.
⚠Saffron Yellow (#FDBE00) on white does not meet WCAG AA — always pair it with Black Coal text, or use as background with dark text.
🚫Disabled states must be visually distinct — use reduced opacity and cursor change, not color alone.
✓ Saffron + Black Coal
✓ Black + White
✓ Dark Grey + White
✗ Saffron + White
10

Do's & Don'ts

Do

  • Use Saffron Yellow as the primary action color for CTAs and active states.
  • Use Black Coal for high-contrast text buttons and selected tabs.
  • Keep input labels above the field, helper text between label and input.
  • Use full rounded pill shapes for buttons, filters, tabs, and tags.
  • Show real-time validation feedback with icons and semantic colors.

Don't

  • Use Red or Green for decorative purposes — reserved for validation only.
  • Place light text on Saffron Yellow — always use Black Coal.
  • Mix border-radius styles — maintain pill shapes across interactive components.
  • Use Saffron Yellow as a text color — fills and backgrounds only.
  • Use China branding colors (Blue Wave, Marigold) outside the China market.
